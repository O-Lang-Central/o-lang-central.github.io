O-Lang Protocol

Open Specification for AI Orchestration

O-Lang is an open, vendor-neutral protocol for auditable, portable, and governable AI orchestration.

Unlike developer-centric frameworks, O-Lang operates at the execution governance layer—enabling organizations to certify, audit, and scale AI workflows across models, tools, and teams.

v1.0 Specification

  • Core Syntax
    Workflow structure, steps, blocks, and symbol binding
  • Semantic Enforcement
    Symbol availability, graceful degradation, and context integrity
  • Policy Enforcement
    Resolver allow-listing, capability isolation, and compliance boundaries
  • Time-Bound Execution
    Parallel timeouts, escalation chains, and deterministic fallbacks
  • Determinism & Auditability
    Execution traces, reproducibility, and audit trails
